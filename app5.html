Gravity Simulator

This app is a simplified version of a fundamental N-body simulator. N-body simulator is to model the motion of a large number of particles which interact with each other through some specific force. In this model we consider Newtonian gravity. You can also plot the energy evolution and pick some particle to check their status. You can press arrows to move around the simulation box when you have magnified the view. Enjoy! :-D
